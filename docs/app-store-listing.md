## English (primary locale: en-US)

**App name (max 30):**
`Serenity: Mood & Calm`

**Subtitle (max 30):**

**Promotional text (max 170, editable anytime):**
`Check in, talk to an AI that remembers your week, and unwind with calming sounds and guided sessions. Private by design — your data stays on your device.`

**Keywords (max 100, comma-separated, no spaces):**
`mood,journal,anxiety,calm,meditation,cbt,sleep,gratitude,wellbeing,mindfulness,self care,diary,breathe,habits`

**Description:**
```
Serenity is a calm, private space for your mind — a mood journal, an AI companion that actually remembers you, and tools that help when things get hard.

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT
• An AI that remembers. Your chat, insights and weekly reports are built on your real history — recurring themes, how you've been sleeping, what lifts or weighs on your mood.
• See your patterns. Mood check-ins with a tactile energy/tension pad and an emotion wheel reveal what truly affects how you feel — including links to your sleep and activity from Apple Health.

EVERY DAY
• Quick mood check-ins with personal AI insights
• A supportive AI chat, available any time
• Calming soundscapes (rain, ocean, forest) and short guided meditations
• A focus timer with ambient sound
• Journal, gratitude, and daily affirmations

TOOLS THAT HELP
• Reframe an anxious thought with gentle, CBT-informed guidance
• Build a personal "when it hits" coping plan
• Track small habits and see how they connect to your mood

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Your entries stay on your device. No account, no ads, no trackers, no selling your data. Apple Health is read-only and optional.

Serenity is a supportive wellness tool, not a substitute for professional care.
```
